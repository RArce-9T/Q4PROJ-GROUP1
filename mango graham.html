Mango Graham


This mango graham recipe is inspired by Jean Reynaert. His mango graham was his choice of food to the Panlasang Pinoy Recipe Contest few years ago.
Prep: 10mins
Cook: 1min
Total: 11mins

Ingredients


1 tablespoon of crushed graham
20 pcs graham crackers
1 cup all purpose cream or coolwhip
3/4 cup condensed milk
2 cups of mangoes (thinly sliced)


Cooking Instructions


1.In a rectangular dish, arrange 8 to 10 pieces of graham crackers. Set aside.
2. In a bowl, combine the cream/cool whip and condensed milk and mix well.
3. On the layered crackers on the bottom, spread the milk and cream mixture.
4. Then spread out the thin sliced mangoes evenly on top of the cream.
5. Make another layer of graham crackers; spread the cream and mango slices. (You can do lots of layers if you want)
6. Garnish the top layer with mango and sprinkle the crushed graham
7. Chill for at least 3 hours before serving.
